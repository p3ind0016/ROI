Economic Value Analysis

Welcome to ESG's Economic Value Tool for EMC InfoArchive

This interactive calculator is designed to help determine the relative costs and benefits of decommissioning and actively archiving an array of applications with EMC InfoArchive compared to a present mode of operation or status quo which assumes some applications are kept active to serve as an archive and other applications rely on their backups as an archive.

To begin an analysis, proceed to the inputs section and provide information about your InfoArchive use case. Next, review your results on the report screens. You can return to the inputs or model administration tabs at any time and modify any or all of your entries.
